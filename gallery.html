Module Gallery

The following modules are currently published in the main CitySDK repository:

Census Bureau APIs

citysdk.census.js

Leverage the powerful data of the U.S. Census Bureau by implementing the Census module into your application. Access data from the American Community Survey and integrate it with GeoJSON features from TigerWeb.

Energy Information Agency

citysdk.eia.js

Access the extensive datasets of the U.S. Energy Information Agency using this module. Browse various categories and use their data series in your visualizations and analyses.

USDA Farmer's Market Directory

citysdk.farmersMarket.js

Discover a world of fresh food near you! By integrating the U.S. Department of Agriculture's National Farmer's Market directory module, you can find markets near any location in the U.S. including details such as products and services offered as well as hours of operation.

Socrata

citysdk.socrata.js

Infuse your application with open data, powered by Socrata. Socrata is one of the leading applications municipal, state, and federal agencies use to distribute their data. Using the Socrata module is a snap, making the incorporation of open data into your development a breeze.
